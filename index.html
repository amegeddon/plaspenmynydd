Plas Penmynydd

Birthplace of a Royal Dynasty

Proudly bearing the Tudor coat of arms, Plas Penmynydd, the birthplace of Owain Tudor, is the ancestral home of the Tudor dynasty.

Opening Times


Plas Penmynydd is a private home to a busy household, however, we are passionate about sharing the unique history of the house with as many people as possible.

The house is open to the public every last Sunday of the month from 12-5pm.

Last admission is 4pm.
